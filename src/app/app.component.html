Delicious Bread Recipe

Rating: 4.5/5

Preparation Time: 2 hours

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp yeast

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water to the dry ingredients and mix well to form a dough.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes, then let it rise for an hour.
